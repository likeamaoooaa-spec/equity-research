## Summary

**Palantir Technologies**([PLTR](https://www.fool.com/quote/nasdaq/pltr/)+2.56%) reported its strongest quarterly revenue and operational metrics as a public company, with eleven consecutive quarters of accelerating revenue growth. ## Industry glossary

*   **AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform)**: Palantir Technologies' integrated enterprise AI platform that governs, coordinates, and executes agentic workflows on structured and unstructured data, emphasizing auditable, regulated “no-slop” operational performance.
*   **Ontology**: The unified data schema underpinning AIP and Foundry, enabling precise governance, traceability, and actionable insights for enterprise AI workflows.
*   **FDE (Foundry Developer Environment)**: Palantir Technologies’ suite of tools allowing for custom, extensible data pipeline and application development within its software platforms.
*   **Ship OS**: An operational software system jointly deployed with the U.S. Department of the Navy, streamlining manufacturing and supply chain processes.
*   **Maven Smart System**: A battlefield AI and data fusion platform for the U.S. Department of Defense, supporting real-time intelligence and operational command.
*   **AIFD (AI Factory Developer)**: Palantir Technologies’ developer platform for deploying and operating AI-powered applications in government and defense contexts.
*   **TCV (Total Contract Value)**: The aggregate dollar value of contracted agreements, including the full committed term of all signed deals.
*   **Rule of 40 score**: A combined metric representing the sum of revenue growth rate and adjusted operating margin, widely used in software/SaaS analysis to benchmark operating performance.
*   **Zero day**: A cybersecurity term for newly discovered, exploitable vulnerabilities in software that require immediate remediation before being widely recognized or patched.
